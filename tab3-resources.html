Resources

Below are a list of different organizations that are helping the Yemen crisis. All of the websites have a donation page on their website.

    Mona Relief
  • This organization is founded by Fatik Abudullah Al-Rodaini who is a journalist in Yemen. This group focuses on humanitarian relief and development.
  • They buy food from local vendors and prepare baskets of food for families that need them. They have helped over 43,000 people.
    Yemen Hope & Relief
  • Ahmad Algohbary was impacted by the war in Yemen deeply ever since he lost his friends because of it. After losing so many people and feeling hopeless, he decided to create this organization to help others.
  • Algohbary captured pictures of people facing the harsh repercussions of the war and began posting them on Twitter. Once people saw the people suffering, they decided to donate which started the aid relief.
  • During Ramadan, Algobary was able to donate food packages to 50 families who were forced to move during the war.
    International Rescue Committee (IRC)
  • This organization has the goal of assisting people who have been impacted by conflicts and issues that are out of their control by helping them get control of their life again.
  • The IRC is continuing to help Yemen in several ways which include providing water and sanitation services, medical supplies, training the staff on cholera treatment, and much more.
    INTERSOS
  • This Rome based organization has been helping Yemen since 2008 and hasn't stopped since. Ever since the war began, they have helped thousands of people and provided medical and food assistance
  • They have helped 125 people in 2018 through mobile clinics.
    Oxfam
  • Oxfam is an organization that has the mission to help end proverty and help people all over the world.
  • Oxfam provides necessities such water and sanitations to even the most remote places in Yemen
